About HAMK

HAMK is a smart donation-connect platform that links donors of clothes/items with NGOs so that items reach the needy instead of going to waste.

Our Mission

Reduce waste, increase impact, and make donating simple and transparent.

How We Help

  • Nearby NGO discovery using your location
  • Clear donation guidelines
  • Direct connect to NGO for pickup/drop
